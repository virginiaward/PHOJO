1968 Democratic National Convention - Chicago

About 10,000 protesters gathered outside the Democratic National Convention in Chicago.

The above picture was shot by Leonard McCombe. This photo depicts the how large the protest was, as well as the division in the country with a clearly drawn line between the civilians and police.

Photojournalism and the media depicted to the entire country how violent protests were becoming.

By: Madeline McKernan, Virginia Ward, Victoria, Cheyne and Anthony Ruffner
